Californium Brewing and Winemaking

Welcome to CBAW! We are the premier amateur fermentation club at UC Berkeley.

As a Registered Student Organization, we have access to University resources for general meetings.

Our signature events are our BREW CREWS: experienced brewers host a hands-on homebrewing event for a small group of club members. The best way to learn is to do!

Additionally, we'll have:

  • Seminars on brewing basics, and the history and science behind food fermentation
  • Invited speakers from industry and academia
  • Field trips to local brewing operations*
  • Beer and wine tasting events*

*not yet 21? We can't serve you alcohol, but don't despair: you can join a Brew Crew and attend other events!
